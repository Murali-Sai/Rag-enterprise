OUTLOOK

Management expects continued momentum in investment banking as the M&A pipeline remains robust heading into 2025. Wealth management is expected to benefit from secular trends in fee-based advisory. Net interest income may face headwinds from potential rate cuts. The CRE portfolio remains an area of focus with active risk management.
