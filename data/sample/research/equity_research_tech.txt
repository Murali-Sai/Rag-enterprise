INDUSTRY OUTLOOK: OVERWEIGHT

The technology sector enters 2025 with strong fundamentals driven by enterprise AI adoption, cloud migration, and cybersecurity spending. We raise our sector rating to Overweight from Market Weight.

KEY THEMES FOR 2025:

1. Enterprise AI Spending
- Enterprise AI spending projected to reach $180B in 2025 (up 35% YoY)
- Infrastructure (GPUs, cloud compute) represents 55% of spend
- Software/platforms represent 30%, services 15%
- Top beneficiaries: cloud hyperscalers, semiconductor companies, AI platform vendors
- Risk: Commoditization of inference could pressure margins in H2 2025

2. Cloud Computing
- Public cloud market expected to reach $680B in 2025 (up 20% YoY)
- Multi-cloud and hybrid strategies driving demand for cloud management platforms
- Edge computing emerging as growth vector (projected $95B market by 2026)
- AWS market share: 32%, Azure: 24%, GCP: 12%

3. Cybersecurity
- Cybersecurity spending projected at $215B in 2025 (up 14% YoY)
- Zero-trust architecture adoption accelerating
- AI-powered threat detection driving next upgrade cycle
- Regulatory requirements (SEC cyber disclosure rules) creating mandated spending
